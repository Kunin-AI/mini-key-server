FAQ

What exactly is Teacher's Pet?
A desktop app (Mac/Windows) that reads scanned test papers — including handwritten answers — and grades them against your answer key using AI. You define regions on the page, it does the rest.
What does "free for life" actually mean?
Your license never expires and you'll never be charged. When we launch paid tiers, First Bell members keep full access at no cost. Forever.
Do I need special hardware?
Any computer running macOS or Windows. For scanning, a flatbed scanner works best, but phone photos work too. The AI runs on our servers, so your machine doesn't need a GPU.
Is my students' data safe?
Test images are processed and never stored on our servers. Everything stays on your machine. We only collect anonymized usage metrics (e.g., "graded 30 tests") to improve the product.
What if I apply and don't get in?
We'll keep your application on file. If we expand the program or spots open up, you'll be first in line.
